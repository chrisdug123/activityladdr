How It Works

Welcome to our activity management app! Here's how you can make the most of it:

1. Sign Up and Log In

Create an account to get started. Once you're logged in, you can access all features of the app.

2. Link Your Strava Account

Connect your Strava account to import your activities automatically. This allows the app to calculate totals and let you climb the laddr!

3. Earn points by exercising!

Keep exercising ! Exercising will earn you points and the more points you have, the greater chance you have to win the end of month raffle!

Earning Points

  • Riding : Earn 0.1 points per kilometer hour (e.g. 20 kilometers at 30km/h, is 600 kilometer hours, so 60 points)
  • Running : Earn 1 points per kilometer hour (e.g. 5 kilometers at 5 minutes per kilometer (12km/h), is 60 kilometer hours, so 60 points)

Interacting with events earns your more points!

  • Public Event : Check the schedule for public events! There is 1 schedule for all users, align your activities with these events for a 10x multiplier
  • Personal Event : Activate yourself and all of your activities for 24hrs will receive a 3x multiplier

4. Earn Bucks

Each week, you receive 5 bucks, which you can use to schedule public events or for personal event activation. Plan your activities wisely to make the most of them!
